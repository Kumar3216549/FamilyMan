The Dispatcher's Lantern Ravi worked late nights in a small logistics office, where endless paperwork and phone calls blurred together. One evening, when a storm knocked out the power, he lit a lantern and kept coordinating shipments by hand. His calm persistence ensured that medicines reached a hospital on time. The next morning, his manager said, “You didn’t just move goods — you carried hope.” From then on, Ravi realized his work wasn’t just about boxes and bills, but about lives depending on his quiet dedication.
